Grilled Cheese

Description

Grilled cheese is a childhood favorite of almost everyone I know. It is easy to make and tasty to eat. The melted cheese and crispy bread pair so nicely, it could only be better with a bowl of Hot Tomato Soup.

While any bread will do, adding something fancy here can have some pretty fantastic results. I like a Rosemary Olive Oil bread for something special. Experiment for yourself too see what would make your grilled cheese Special!

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Butter or Mayo
  • Cheese of choice

Steps:

  1. Warm a medium size pan over medium heat.
  2. With a knife, spread butter or Mayo on one side of two pieces of bread. Place the slathered sides together.
  3. Place cheese of choice on top of the pieces of bread.
  4. Once the pan is warm, Carefully seperate the bottom slice of bread from the pile and place the other piece of bread onto the pan. Now you can place the bottom slice on top of the cheese with the butter or mayo facing upward.
  5. Let the sandwich cook until the bottom piece of bread is toasted brown and crispy. Using a spatula, flip the sandwich over onto the pan.
  6. Allow the sandwich to cook another 3-4 minutes, until the second piece of bread is brown and crispy.
  7. Finally, remove the sandwich from the pan and turn off the heat.
